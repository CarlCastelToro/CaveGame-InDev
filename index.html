CaveGame is a 3D voxel survival game built with Three.js.

Mine blocks, craft tools, manage your oxygen, and survive the dangers lurking in the dark caves.

Controls: WASD to move, Space to jump, Left Click to mine, Right Click to place, Scroll/1-9 to select items, I for crafting grid.

Don't click the title.
